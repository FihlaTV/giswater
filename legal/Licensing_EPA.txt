TERMS OF USE OF EPANET

EPANET is a program for analyzing the hydraulic and water quality behavior of pressurized pipe networks. It was developed by the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency's National Risk Management Research Laboratory. As such, it is public domain software that may be freely copied and distributed. A complete Users Manual as well as full source code and other updates can be downloaded from ww.epa.gov/ORD/NRMRL/wswrd/epanet.html.
